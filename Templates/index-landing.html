Cardiology

Appasamy Hospital represents to the most modern cardiac care facilities which are affordable and available to all. Our dedicated team of cardiologists and cardiac surgeons is well-known for their expertise, knowledge, and vast experience in their respective arena of heart related problems. Appasamy Hospital offers a full range of adult cardiology and cardiac surgery treatments, to help in the early disease detection and timely treatment of varying heart ailments.

Nephrology

Kidney problems are reportedly increasing in India. Caring for such patients require a blend of technology, quality nursing and clinical expertise. Appasamy Hospitals is well equipped to offer comprehensive care to patients with kidney problems. Nephrology treats all kinds of renal diseases including acute and chronic renal diseases due to diabetes mellitus, hypertension, stone disease and infections.

Orthopaedics

Appasamy Hospital offers the latest technique and treatment for injuries and diseases of musculoskeletal system-bones, joints, ligaments, tendons and muscles. The services we offers are surgical care for bone, joint problems resulting from injuries, infections, nutritional or metabolic disorders. We are well-known hospital in Chennai for knee replacement surgeries.

Urology

Appasamy Hospital is happy to announce that we are collaborated with Tamil Nadu Urological Research Center Ltd(TNURC- Kidney Stone Clinic)one of the well-known and most preferred lithotripsy centre for all urologists in Chennai We at Appasamy hospital, offers comprehensive care for various urological conditions. We have state-of-the-art facilities and specialists in providing comprehensive care in the areas of stone disease, UTI’s, uro-oncology, reconstructive urology, minimal invasive procedure like laparoscopic surgery and renal transplantation
